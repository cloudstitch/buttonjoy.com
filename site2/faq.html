Buttonjoy FAQ

Magic Buttons

Can I change my notifications?

Yes! When your button ships, you will receive an email with instructions to log in and modify your notification settings if you ever want to.

Charity Buttons

How does the donation work?

We use Stripe, the best payment processor in Silicon Valley, to securely store your credit details when you purchase your button. When you press the button, we issue a new charge to that card for the amount that you selected.

What if I accidentally click the button?

When you click the charity button, we'll send you a text message asking for confirmation. We only charge your card and make the donation if you send back a confirmation.

Is my donation tax deductible?

Not yet. Buttonjoy is a part of Cloudstitch Inc., a C-Corporation. Since we act as an intermediary for your donations, they can not be deducted from taxes. For the small, regular donations Buttonjoy encourages, this probably works just fine for you. If you would like to use Buttonjoy to donate larger amounts, please reach out to us.

We're working on a payment solution that passes donations directly from you to your designated charity, which would make them fully tax deductible to you.

How much of each donation goes to charity?

When you click a charity button and we charge your card, Buttonjoy sends 95% of the money we receive to the charity you designated. We retain 5% to help us pay for processing costs and technical infrastructure.

Why do you charge for the charity button?

We're selling the charity button at cost -- and we'll keep working to drive that cost as low as possible!

Setup

How do I use my button when I get it?

Your button ships ready to use. The only step necessary is to connect it to your WiFi network. When your button ships, we will email you a link to a set of easy instructions.

Can I take my button wherever I go?

You can, but you have to connect it to a WiFi network in order to use it. This takes about a minute.

Will the battery run out?

Eventually. We use Amazon's stock IoT buttons, which have an expected life of 1000 clicks.

Privacy

Will you share my personal information with charities?

Only if you ask us to, and even then, only to the charity you have designated. There is a checkbox to opt-in to information sharing when you purchase your button.

Sharing your information enables these groups to reach out and say thank you. In many cases, you will probably also be subscribed to their regular mailings.

Will you share my personal information with anybody else?

Not unless we are required to by law. We're not in the business of selling your data and never will be.

Technical Details

Who supplies the buttons?

We pre-purchase Amazon's off-the-shelf IoT Buttons, configure them to speak with our cloud services, and provide you with an easy online tool to control what happens when you push them.

Are these buttons "hacked"?

No -- we are using buttons manufactured by Amazon for the explicit purpose of customizing to do interesting things like this.
